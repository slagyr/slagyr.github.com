Module Limelight::Builtin::Players::PasswordBox
In: lib/limelight/builtin/players/password_box.rb

A Builtin Player that adds the look and behavior of a native password box. It may be applied in the PropBuilder DSL like so: 

  my_password_box :players => "password_box"

Props including this Player may implement any of the key and focus event hooks: 

  key_pressed, key_typed, key_released, focus_gained, focus_lost

[Validate]
